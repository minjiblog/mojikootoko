About Me

I'm MINJI (also known as "mojikootoko"), an amateur photographer living in Japan. I love Mojiko, a charming port town here, and I want to share some of the photos I've taken. Through my pictures, I hope you can feel the warm sunshine, the peaceful sea, and the beauty of everyday life.
